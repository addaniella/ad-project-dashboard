⚙ Setup — Connect Your Google Sheet

1. Open your Google Sheet → File → Share → Publish to web → Entire Document → Publish
2. Copy the spreadsheet ID from the URL bar (the long string between /d/ and /edit)
3. Paste it below and click Connect

Your sheet must have tabs named exactly: projects, tasks, action_items (matching the template)
